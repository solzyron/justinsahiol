ANAHAW PERFUME

  • The sweet scent that flows from the leaves of the dried anahaw placed on the beams of small huts as roofing reveals the preciousness of age that gives off a sweet smell as it grows older and brittle.) As it comes drifting to my nose, I am thrown back to years ago when I was but a little child at play. Should I continue this poem? The bells are ringing… Well, anyway, the scent of dried anahaw leaves gives me the feeling of knowing better now that I have advanced in age… Back when I owned my time and I was yet young, I give off not a sweet smell. Now that I am a bit older, I know I’ve given off more…
